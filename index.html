Directed by Brady Meyer, the Clear Brook High School Orchestra program offers the prospect of a high school musical journey. The various concerts, solos, and the experience of working together in an orchestra for the next 4 years enables students to develop their capabilities in an environment that both supports their artistic passions and enables them to socialize with their fellow musicians.
